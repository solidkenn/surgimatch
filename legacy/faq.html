SurgiMatch – Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how we match you, our fees, privacy, and what to expect.

What is SurgiMatch?

SurgiMatch is a patient-matching service that connects you with trusted surgeons and clinics based on your needs, budget, and preferences. We simplify the process so you don’t have to spend hours researching on your own.

How does SurgiMatch work?

You submit your details through our form, and we assess your needs. We then match you with suitable clinics or surgeons and help guide you through the next steps, including consultations and booking.

Is SurgiMatch free for patients?

We offer a Basic Match (Free) option and a Premium Concierge (Paid) service. A service fee applies for personalised matching and end-to-end support, in line with the plan you select—so you can choose the level of help that fits you.

How do you choose the surgeons or clinics?

We work with carefully selected clinics and qualified surgeons. Our recommendations are based on your specific needs, procedure type, and preferences.

Do you guarantee results?

No. SurgiMatch does not perform procedures or guarantee outcomes. All medical procedures are carried out by independent surgeons. We focus on helping you find the right match and making the process easier.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously and handle your information in accordance with Australian privacy laws. Your details are only shared with relevant clinics as part of the matching process after obtaining your consent. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

Am I obligated to book a procedure?

No. There is no obligation to proceed. You are free to explore your options and decide what’s right for you.

Can I choose my own surgeon?

Yes. You are always in control. We provide recommendations, but the final decision is completely yours.

What types of procedures do you help with?

We assist with a range of procedures including orthopaedics, cosmetic, elective, and other surgical services. Contact us to see how we can help with your specific needs.

Do I need a referral?

This depends on the procedure and clinic. Some medical procedures may require a GP referral, while cosmetic procedures typically do not.

How long does the process take?

Most patients receive matches within 24–72 hours after submitting their details.

Why should I use SurgiMatch instead of searching myself?

We save you time, reduce overwhelm, and provide guidance based on real clinical experience—helping you make more informed decisions with confidence.

Still have questions?

Submit your details and we’ll guide you through everything step-by-step.
