Privacy Policy

How SurgiMatch collects, uses, and protects your personal information under the Privacy Act 1988 (Australia).

SurgiMatch ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Australia).

1. What information we collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location
  • Procedure(s) of interest
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We do not require detailed medical history to use our service.

2. How we collect your information

We collect information when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through our website
  • Contact us directly via social media, phone, or email

3. Why we collect your information

We collect your information to:

  • Match you with relevant surgical clinics
  • Facilitate consultations with providers
  • Improve our services

4. Sharing your information

By using SurgiMatch, you agree that:

  • Your information may be shared with relevant clinics or healthcare providers
  • These providers may contact you directly regarding your enquiry

We only share your information with providers relevant to your request.

5. Data storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access.

6. Access and correction

You may request access to or correction of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact:
info@surgimatch.com.au

By using our services, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
